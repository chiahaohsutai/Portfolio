My Background

A short introduction to my person

Hello, my name is Chia Hao or David. I'm a second year Northeastern student pursuing a degree in Data Science and Mathematics. I have always been immerse in a multicultural enviorment and I belive it has shaped my perspective on the world and diversity. I was born and raised in Uruguay to Chinese immigrants exposing me to contrasting customs and habits (Latino and Chinese). Furthermore, I attended an International school (Uruguayan American School) and made friends from across the World, lifting my cultural awareness more. I also have a strong background in varsity team sports. I partook in the volleyball, soccer and basketball varsity teams and I had the opportunity to travel and compete at a regional level. My involment in team sports has helped me develop my character in mutiple ways, especially in the areas of dicipline, teamwork and time-management.

I found my passion for programming very recently. About a year ago, I was on track to graduate as an Industrial Engineer. Yet, after being exposed to different programming languages, I knew that my interest were not in engineering but is coding. I found myself captivated by the complexity algorithms, the logical approach to design and the freedom to create. Moreover, programming has offered me a space where I'm constantly learning and taking on new challenges.
